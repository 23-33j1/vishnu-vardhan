About IRD/TD

The IRD/TD department is the hub for fostering innovation, research, and advanced technical training at our institution. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and industry through collaborative projects, professional development, and hands-on training programs.

Objectives

  • Promote interdisciplinary research across departments.
  • Collaborate with industries and research bodies.
  • Offer training programs to enhance student and staff skills.
  • Support innovation and entrepreneurship initiatives.

Ongoing Projects

  • AI-Powered Agriculture Monitoring System
  • Smart City Waste Management Solutions
  • IoT-based Health Monitoring Devices

  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals

  • Startup and Innovation Challenges
